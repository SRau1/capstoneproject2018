About Our App

Lie To Me is an application meant to train the user to become a better lie detector. Though this app does not provide a guaranteed answer, the user's inputs are evaluated by an algorithm developed by our research and evaluations from academic studies. Lie To Me provides quizzes that help users learn more in depth what each tell could mean and other varying factors that could influence the user's results.
